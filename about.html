Our story Latest news

Built from the ground up.

We focus on practical, quality glass and aluminum work delivered with care, consistency, and respect for each client’s needs.

JTH Glass and Aluminum Works was established in 2024 and continues to grow one project at a time.

We serve residential and commercial clients with custom glass and aluminum solutions based on clear communication, proper measurement, and careful execution.

As a young business, we are still building our track record. Our goal is simple: do honest work, improve continuously, and earn long-term trust through reliable service.

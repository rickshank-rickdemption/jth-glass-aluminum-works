Frequently Asked Questions

Is the instant quotation final?

+
The instant quotation is an estimate based on the product, size, and quantity you entered. Final pricing is confirmed after site measurement and technical checking.

What services are included?

+
Most projects include fabrication, supply, and installation of glass and aluminum works. The exact scope is shown in your quotation and confirmed during booking.

How long does production and installation usually take?

+
Lead time depends on your selected system, glass type, and site readiness. After confirmation, we provide a target schedule for production and installation.

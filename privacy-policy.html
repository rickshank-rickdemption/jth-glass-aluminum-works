Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

We collect only the information needed to process quotations, bookings, and customer support requests.

1. Data We Collect

  • Name, email, phone number, and address (when provided)

  • Inquiry messages and booking-related updates

2. How We Use Data

  • Prepare estimates and respond to inquiries
  • Coordinate site visits, fabrication, and installation schedules
  • Send service-related updates and customer support responses

3. Retention

Inquiry and booking records are retained for up to 12 months from the latest activity, unless a longer period is required by law.

4. Protection and Access

We apply reasonable technical and operational safeguards. Access to internal admin actions is controlled and logged for security and operational review.

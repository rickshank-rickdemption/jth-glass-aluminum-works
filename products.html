Glass Specifications.

Material options for different performance needs, from everyday installations to safety, insulation, and heat-control requirements.

Safety

Tempered Glass

Heat-treated for 4x strength. Shatters into safe granules. Standard for doors & showers.

Security

Laminated Glass

Two layers bonded with PVB. Stays intact when broken. Ideal for canopies and railings.

Energy

Low-E Glass

Low-emissivity coating that reflects heat while letting light in. Energy efficient.

Comfort

Insulated Glass

Double glazed unit with air space. Provides superior sound and heat insulation.

Solar Control

Tinted / Reflective

Reduces glare and solar heat gain. Available in Bronze, Grey, Green, and Blue.

Standard

Ordinary / Annealed

Standard glass. Can be cut and processed. Used for basic windows and louvers.
